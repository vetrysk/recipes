Sweet Payasam

Steps to make sweet payasam:

Step1: make ready your roasted vermicili (250gm)

Step2: Add ghee & vermicili in a pan and heat it for 5 min

Step3: Then add some cashewnuts and rasins, fry it along with vermicili

Step4: Add 0.5 liters of milk and 100gms of sugar in pan along with vermicili

Step5: Heat it for 10-15 mins

And That's it your sweet payasm is ready!!!!
